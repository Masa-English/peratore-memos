📘 宿題 — Pattern Practice

FPP = 相手のセリフ（患者さん）／ SPP = あなたのセリフ（受付スタッフ）

セット 1　時間を提案する表現：How about + 時間 + 曜日?
Pair 1FPPI'd like to come in to see a doctor sometime soon.
SPPWhen would you like to make an appointment?
Pair 2FPPThursday, if possible.
SPPHow about 11 am on Thursday?
Pair 3FPPThat works. Thank you.
SPPGreat. I'll book that for you.
セット 2　時間を提案する表現：How about + 時間 + today/tomorrow?
Pair 1FPPI'd like to make an appointment.
SPPHow about 3 pm today?
Pair 2FPPSorry, I can't today. How about tomorrow?
SPPOkay. How about 11 am tomorrow?
Pair 3FPPI can do that. Thank you!
SPPNo problem. I'll book that for you.
セット 3　症状の確認と予約の進行：ウォークインで空きを待つ場合
Pair 1FPPWhat symptoms do you have?
SPPI have pain..
Pair 2FPPDo you have bleeding?
SPPYes.
Pair 3FPPSince when?
SPPSince yesterday.
Pair 4FPPWhen would you like to make an appointment?
SPPYes please! Today, if possible.
Pair 5FPPI'm sorry, we are fully booked today. You can walk in but there may be a wait.
SPPOkay, thank you. I'll do that.
セット 4　症状の確認と予約の進行：本日の予約（緊急性あり）
Pair 1FPPWhat symptoms do you have?
SPPI have pain..
Pair 2FPPDo you have bleeding?
SPPYes.
Pair 3FPPSince when?
SPPSince yesterday.
Pair 4FPPWell.. Can you come in today?
SPPYes, that'd be great!
Pair 5FPPHow about 3pm?
SPPAny availability after 4pm?
Pair 6FPPWe also have 4:30pm.
SPPPerfect, thank you.
セット 5　症状の確認と予約の進行：後日の予約（緊急性なし）
Pair 1FPPWhat symptoms do you have?
SPPI have pain..
Pair 2FPPDo you have bleeding?
SPPYes.
Pair 3FPPSince when?
SPPSince yesterday.
Pair 4FPPWould you like to make an appointment?
SPPYes please. How about Thursday?
Pair 5FPPYes, we have availability on Thursday. How about 3 pm?
SPPThat works. Thank you very much.

💎 キーフレーズ

We have availability on Thursday.
ウィ　ハヴ　アヴェイラビリティ　オン　サーズデイ
木曜日に空きがございます。
A: Thursday, if possible.
B: We have availability on Thursday. How about 3 pm?
How about + 時間 + on + 曜日 / today?
〜時はいかがですか？
✅  How about 3 pm on Thursday?（曜日 → on が必要）
✅  How about 3 pm today?（today → on は不要）
We also have 4:30 pm.
16時30分にも空きがございます。
✅  We also have 4:30 pm.（also は動詞の前）
❌  We have also 4:30 pm.
We are fully booked today.
本日は予約が満員です。
You can walk in, but there may be a wait.
予約なしでもご来院いただけますが、お待ちいただく場合があります。
